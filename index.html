Introduction

Hello! My name is Mariia Nechepurenko, and I am an exchange student from Saxion University in the Netherlands. I am originally from Ukraine, but in 2022 I moved to Luxembourg, and later, in 2023, I settled in the Netherlands to pursue my studies in Creative Media and Game Technologies. My specialization is 3D visualization for the game industry, though I am now eager to explore the field of technical art more deeply.
I dedicate much of my time to studying, but outside of academics I enjoy building LEGO, engaging in creative projects, and simply relaxing with a good book or film. I am truly excited about the opportunity to spend time in the US, and I look forward to making the most of this experience in the coming months.
